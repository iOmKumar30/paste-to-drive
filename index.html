Paste to Google Drive

Streamline your workflow by uploading clipboard images directly to specific Google Drive folders.

What it Does

Paste to Google Drive is a productivity-focused Chrome Extension designed to eliminate the friction of saving, renaming, and manually moving screenshots. With a single keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V), you can instantly transmit images from your clipboard directly into your designated Google Drive cloud storage folder.

Who it is For

This extension is built for developers, students, researchers, designers, and anyone who frequently captures technical documentation, layout previews, or visual references and needs them cleanly organized in the cloud without cluttering their local downloads directory.

How it Works

  1. Click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar to open the control interface.
  2. Paste your targeted Google Drive Folder ID string into the setup field.
  3. Click "Authorize Google Drive" to securely log into your account via Google's native identity systems.
  4. Click inside the designated canvas zone and press Ctrl + V (or Cmd + V on macOS). Your asset uploads immediately in the background.
